About Water Safety Parameters

pH Level

Measures water acidity/alkalinity. Safe drinking water should be between 6.5 and 8.5.

Turbidity

Indicates water cloudiness. High turbidity can harbor harmful microorganisms.

Chloramines

Common water disinfectant. Levels above 4 ppm can cause health issues.

TDS

Total Dissolved Solids. High levels affect taste and may indicate contamination.
